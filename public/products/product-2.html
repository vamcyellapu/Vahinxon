Vahinxon 15W 40 CI-4 Plus

The Vahinxon Ultra Turbo 15W40 engine oil is a heavy-duty commercial engine oil designed for use in diesel engines. It is formulated with a high viscosity index base oil and incorporates a selection of antioxidant, detergent, and anti-wear additives. This combination of ingredients enables the oil to meet several API (American Petroleum Institute) performance standards, indicating its suitability for different applications. The API classifications it meets are:

API CF-4/SM Multi Grade
API CH-4/SN
API CI-4/SN
These API specifications suggest that the oil is suitable for use in diesel engines and offers various performance benefits, including detergency, anti-wear protection, and the ability to function effectively across a range of temperatures.

The recommended applications for Vahinxon Ultra Turbo 15W40 engine oil include:

Use in T.C. (Truck and Commercial) engines
Diesel engines
Earthmoving and construction equipment
The oil is available in a range of container sizes, providing flexibility for users. These sizes include 1 liter, 2.5 liters, 3 liters, 5 liters, 50 liters, and 210 liters.

When selecting engine oil for your specific vehicle or equipment, it's important to follow the manufacturer's recommendations and adhere to the requirements outlined in your owner's manual to ensure the best performance and protection. Vahinxon Ultra Turbo 15W40 appears to be suitable for heavy-duty applications and diesel engines, as indicated by its API certifications.
