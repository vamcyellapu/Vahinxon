Vahinxon 20W 40 4T

API (American Petroleum Institute) JASO MA2: This API specification is designed for motorcycle oils, and JASO MA2 indicates that it's suitable for wet clutch applications. Semi-synthetic oil typically provides a good balance of performance and cost.

The Viion Ultra 4T Engine Oil is recommended for use in various types of scooters, motorcycles, and mopeds with 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. It's suitable for a range of brands, including TVS, BAJAJ, HERO, HONDA, SUZUKI, YAMAHA, and others

This engine oil is available in multiple container sizes, providing options for different users. The available sizes include 900 ml, 1 liter, 50 liters, and 210 liters.

The Viion Ultra 4T Engine Oil is available in different grades/formats, each with a different viscosity and performance characteristics: 10W30 SN, 10W40 SN, 20W40 SN, 20W50 SN, 15W50 SN The choice of grade/format may depend on the specific requirements of your motorcycle's engine and the climate conditions in which it will be used. It's essential to follow the manufacturer's recommendations for your motorcycle to ensure optimal performance and protection.
